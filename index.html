Our Main Charitable Activities

At Sri Arunagiri Charitable Trust, we are dedicated to transforming lives through impactful initiatives.

Nutritious Meals

Providing nutritious meals for children in rural communities to support their physical and mental development.

Health Assistance

Providing free medication and health services for underprivileged families through local health collaborations.

Mobility Support

Distributing wheelchairs, walking sticks, and mobility aids to help differently-abled individuals regain independence.

Clean Water Access

Installing RO water filters in remote schools and rural regions to ensure access to clean drinking water.

Empowering Education

Providing students with school supplies, uniforms, and access to digital learning tools to support their studies.

Rehabilitation Support

Organizing therapy, assistive technology, and vocational training for those with developmental and physical disabilities.
